Privacy Policy for Mello

Effective date: March 25, 2026

Mello is a productivity and mental wellness app designed to help users build habits, reduce stress, and stay consistent.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information to provide and improve the app:

  • Account information such as your email address
  • User-generated content such as journal entries, habits, stress tracking, and in-app progress
  • App usage information needed to operate features and improve performance
  • Purchase and subscription status through Apple and RevenueCat

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To save your habits, progress, streaks, and customizations
  • To provide premium features and manage subscriptions
  • To maintain app functionality, security, and support

Payments and Subscriptions

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple. Subscription status and purchase-related data may also be processed through RevenueCat to manage access to premium features.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share information with service providers needed to operate the app, such as Supabase for backend services and RevenueCat for subscription management.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the app’s services.

Your Choices

You may contact us if you need help with your account or want your data deleted, subject to applicable law and technical limitations.
